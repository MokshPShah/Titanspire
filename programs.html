overview

Beyond skill development, we foster a love of the game to encourage a lifetime of activity through sports

Eagle Elite in Manhattan offers learn-to-play classes, clinics, competitive teams, drop-ins, and leagues for children and adults. All athletes, from first-time players through seasoned competitors and weekend warriors, can access state-of-the-art athletic facilities, world-class programming, and the highest level of coaching.

Kids

Daycare

The Eagle Elite Daycare Program is for children ages 16 months to 2.6 years. Each Toddler and 2’s classroom have 12 children and 3 teachers. With access to Eagle Elite’ facilities, our toddler and 2’s program offers unique learning opportunities and activities. In natural, authentic ways, we offer students the opportunity to learn and expand their language, cognitive, fine motor, gross motor, and social/emotional skills.
